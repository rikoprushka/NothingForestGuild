How to Play

Press 'F11' to go full screen for a better experience

↑/↓: Change Speed

←/→: Flip Image

Numpad 5: Play Sound

Click/Tap: Toggle Link

H/Spacebar: Toggle This Menu

B: Go to Battle Royale

F: Go to Pixel Fighter

X: Go to @NothingJioo

T: Go to Nothing Forest
